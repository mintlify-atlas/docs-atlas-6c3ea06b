DossPanel's invoice module lets you generate professional invoices, send them to other users, track payment status, and download PDFs for your records.

## Creating an invoice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Invoices">
    Navigate to **Invoices** in the main menu or go to `/invoices`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Create Invoice">
    Click the **Create** button to open the invoice builder at `/invoices/create`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the Bill From section">
    Enter your billing details:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Bill From Name | Your name or business name |
    | Phone Number | Your contact phone number |
    | Description | Short description of your business or role |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the Bill To section">
    Select the recipient from the user list. DossPanel fetches the recipient's full name automatically. The phone number is resolved from the recipient's account (`formattedPhone`).

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Recipient | Select from registered DossPanel users |
    | Bill To Description | Description relevant to the recipient |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add invoice metadata">
    Enter the administrative details:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Invoice ID | Unique reference number for this invoice |
    | Order Number | Related order number (if applicable) |
    | Shipment Number | Shipment tracking reference (if applicable) |
    | Invoice Date | Issue date of the invoice |
    | Due Date | Payment deadline |
    | Shipment Date | Expected shipment or delivery date |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add line items">
    Add one or more items to the invoice. For each item provide:
    - Item Name
    - Item Description
    - Quantity and unit price

    The system calculates the line subtotal automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set financial totals">
    Review and confirm the financial breakdown:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | Subtotal | Sum of all line items |
    | Tax | Tax amount (if applicable) |
    | Discount | Discount to apply |
    | Late Fee | Any late payment charge |
    | Total | Final payable amount |
    | Currency | Currency code for the invoice |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add notes">
    Enter any additional notes, payment terms, or instructions for the recipient in the **Notes** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the invoice">
    Click **Submit**. The invoice is saved with `status = 'unpaid'` and assigned to both the creator and recipient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invoice statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `unpaid` | Invoice created; payment not yet received |
| `paid` | Invoice has been paid |
| `overdue` | Due date has passed and payment has not been received |

## Sending an invoice

After creation, the recipient can be notified via email. The invoice stores the recipient's email and phone number, enabling the platform to send a notification directing them to the invoice payment page.

## Paying an invoice

Recipients who receive an invoice notification can:

1. Click the invoice link in the email.
2. Review the invoice details and total due.
3. Choose a payment method (Stripe, wallet transfer, etc.) to pay the invoice amount.
4. Confirm payment — the invoice status updates to `paid`.

## My Invoices list

All invoices you have created are listed at `/invoices` (the `viewAll` method). Each row shows:

- Invoice ID and order number
- Recipient name
- Total and currency
- Status
- Due date

## Downloading a PDF

From the invoice detail page, click the **Download PDF** button to generate and download a formatted PDF version of the invoice for your records or for sharing offline.

<Tip>
Use the Order Number and Shipment Number fields to cross-reference invoices with your order management or shipping system.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="Can I edit an invoice after creating it?">
Edit functionality depends on the invoice status. Invoices that have already been paid should not be modified — create a credit note or a new corrected invoice instead.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if the due date passes?">
The invoice status can be updated to `overdue`. Late fees configured at the time of creation will appear on the invoice. Contact the recipient to arrange payment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the recipient notified automatically?">
The platform can send an email to the recipient using the `billToPhoneNumber` and recipient email on file when the invoice is created. Check with your administrator whether automatic invoice emails are enabled.
</Accordion>
