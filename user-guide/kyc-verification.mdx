Know Your Customer (KYC) verification confirms your identity and address. Completing KYC is often required before you can access higher transaction limits, certain payment methods, or withdrawal features.

## Why KYC is required

DossPanel operates as a financial platform subject to anti-money-laundering (AML) and regulatory requirements. Verifying your identity and address helps the platform:

- Confirm you are who you say you are.
- Comply with financial regulations in applicable jurisdictions.
- Protect all users from fraudulent activity.
- Unlock higher transaction limits or specific features gated behind verified status.

## Verification types

DossPanel uses two separate verification flows, both stored in the `document_verifications` table with a `verification_type` field.

## Accepted identity documents

The `identity_type` field on the `DocumentVerification` model records which document type you submitted. Accepted options include:

- **National ID**
- **Passport**
- **Driver's License**

## Accepted address documents

For address proof, provide a recent document (typically less than 3 months old) that shows your full name and residential address:

- Utility bill (electricity, water, gas, internet)
- Bank or credit card statement
- Official government correspondence

## How to submit identity verification

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Identity Verification">
    Go to **Profile → Personal ID** or navigate to `/profile/personal-id`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your document type">
    Choose from the `identity_type` dropdown: National ID, Passport, or Driver's License.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your document number">
    Type your full document number in the `identity_number` field. This field is required.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload the document file">
    Attach a clear scan or photo of your document. Accepted file formats are: `pdf`, `png`, `jpg`, `jpeg`, `gif`, `bmp`. The file is stored in `uploads/user-documents/identity-proof-files/`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Click **Submit**. Your verification is saved with `status = 'pending'` and your `identity_verified` flag is set to `false` while the review is underway. You will receive a notification when the administrator processes your submission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you submit a new document while a previous submission is pending or rejected, the old file is replaced and the status resets to `pending`.
</Note>

## Verification statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `pending` | Document received; awaiting admin review |
| `approved` | Verification accepted; limits and features unlocked |
| `rejected` | Document not accepted; you may resubmit with a corrected document |

## Address verification

The address verification flow is separate from identity verification but follows the same process. Navigate to the address proof section of your profile, upload a supported document, and wait for admin approval. The record is stored in `document_verifications` with `verification_type = 'address'`.

## Frequently asked questions

<Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
Verification is processed manually by administrators. Typical review times vary but you will receive an email or platform notification once the review is complete.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My document was rejected — what do I do?">
Resubmit with a clearer image or a different document type. Ensure the document is not expired, all four corners are visible, and the name matches your registered account name.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to verify my address separately from identity?">
Yes. Identity and address are two separate verification types stored as distinct records. You may be required to complete both depending on your jurisdiction or the features you want to access.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I update my document after it has been approved?">
Yes. Submitting a new document resets your `identity_verified` flag to `false` and the status to `pending` until the new document is reviewed.
</Accordion>
