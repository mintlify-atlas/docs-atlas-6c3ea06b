Your profile page is the central place to manage your personal information, change your password, update your phone number, and configure two-step verification preferences.

Navigate to **Profile** from the top navigation or go directly to `/profile`.

## Profile overview

The profile page surfaces the following information at a glance:

- Full name, email address, and registered phone number
- Current default wallet and currency
- Your personal QR code (for receiving payments)
- Two-step verification status
- KYC / identity verification status

## Updating personal information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the profile page">
    Click your avatar or username in the top navigation and select **Profile**, or navigate to `/profile`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit your details">
    Update fields such as first name, last name, address, city, state, country, and timezone. The available fields are sourced from the `user_details` table and include `address_1`, `address_2`, `city`, `state`, `country_id`, and `timezone`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click the **Save** button. A success notification confirms the update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uploading a profile picture

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on your current avatar">
    On the profile page, click the profile image area or the upload button to open the file picker.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select an image file">
    Accepted formats: `jpg`, `jpeg`, `png`, `gif`, `bmp`. The file is validated against these extensions before upload.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm upload">
    The image is saved to `uploads/user-profile/` and a thumbnail is also stored in `uploads/user-profile/thumb/`. The profile page reloads showing your new avatar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Invalid image formats are rejected with the message *"Invalid Image Format!"*. Make sure your file is a standard JPEG, PNG, GIF, or BMP before uploading.
</Note>

## Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request an OTP">
    On the profile page, select the **Change Password** section and click the button to receive a one-time passcode on your registered phone number.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the OTP">
    A 6-digit code is dispatched via the `SendOtpCode` job to your `formattedPhone`. Enter all six digits in the verification inputs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the new password">
    Once the OTP is verified, enter and confirm your new password. The system hashes the password using `Hash::make()` before storing it. A success message confirms the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
There is currently no requirement to provide your old password when changing to a new one. Ensure you are using a trusted device and are logged into the correct account before performing this action.
</Warning>

## Managing your phone number

Your phone number is used for two-step verification, OTP delivery, and peer-to-peer transfers.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add a phone number">
    If no phone number is on file, open **Profile → Phone**, enter the number in international format (e.g. `+12015550123`), and click **Save**. If phone verification is enabled, a 6-digit SMS code is sent; enter it to confirm ownership.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Update your phone number">
    Enter the new number. The system checks it is not already registered to another account. An SMS code is sent to the new number to complete verification.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Remove your phone number">
    Click **Delete** next to your current phone number. This also clears the stored `phone_verification_code`. Note: removing your phone disables SMS-based two-step verification.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Notification preferences

From the profile page you can also adjust whether you receive email or SMS notifications for transaction events. See the [Notifications](/user-guide/notifications) page for full details on the available notification types.

## Two-step verification

The profile page links to the Two-Factor Authentication settings. You can configure the verification type (email, phone/SMS, or Google Authenticator) from **Profile → Security**. For the full setup guide, see [Two-Factor Authentication](/user-guide/two-factor-auth).

## Default wallet

The profile page displays your current default wallet. This wallet is used as the pre-selected currency when initiating transfers, deposits, or exchanges. You can change the default from the wallet section of your profile by selecting a different fiat currency wallet and saving.
